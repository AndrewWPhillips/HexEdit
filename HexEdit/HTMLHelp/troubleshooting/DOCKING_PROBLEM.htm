Modeless dialog(s) cannot be undocked or are not visible

 

If the registry settings that keep track of where modeless dialogs are positioned (floating position, docked status etc) becomes messed up then they may be docked at the top of the window and not be able to be undocked.  Typically all modeless dialogs are docked and cannot be undocked or moved.

 

This can be easily fixed using the Restore Dialogs Command.  This undocks all modeless dialogs and repositions them at the top-left of the HexEdit window.
